Hello Nikith!

The drum with the letter "w" on it is called the tom tom.

The drum with the letter "a" on it is also a tom tom and sounds lower (lower pitch). Some people have only one tom and others prefer two.

The drum with the letter "s" on it is called the floor tom.

The drum with the letter "d" on it is another floor tom (nobody has two but this is just for fun).

The drum with the letter "j" on it is called the snare drum and is used for things like drum rolls.

The drum with the letter "k" on it is called the crash.

The drum with the letter "l" on it is called the bass drum which creates a loud, booming, deep sound.

For a cool beat, try hitting k & l at the same time, then just k, then j & k at the same time, then just k again. Then you can add in some cool solos (called fills in drums) by using w, a, s, and d!
